Who am I?

Vanessa Rieven is a fictional name, never liked having my name around the Internet, but hi, I'm Vanessa (for real). I'm a weirdo who loves to do way too much stuff, from drawing to 3D Modeling to programing and Web Design, making art and studying more will always be part of me. I'm also a nerd (really?) and I like filling my walls with Games and Metal bands posters. Oh! And I drink a lot of tea, like, a lot...
